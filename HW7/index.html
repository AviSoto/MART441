Social Justice ViewMaster

For my homework assignment, I created a virtual "ViewMaster" viewer that displayed images related to social justice. I searched for images that represented social justice issues and included their title, description, author, and date taken. Each time you press a button, a new image with its information appears. To achieve this, I created a class and multiple objects from it, which were stored in an array. It was a challenging task, but it helped me improve my programming skills while exploring important social justice topics.
